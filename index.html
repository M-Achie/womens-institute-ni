About Us

The Women's Institute NI is dedicated to empowering women through education, advocacy, and community support. We strive to enrich lives and foster a supportive network across Northern Ireland.

Our Programs

Community Outreach

We organize various events and initiatives to support local communities and address pressing issues.

Educational Workshops

Our workshops cover a range of topics from personal development to professional skills enhancement.

Advocacy & Support

We advocate for women's rights and provide support for those facing challenges in their personal or professional lives.
